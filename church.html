We are a Bible-believing, Christ-centered family of all ages. Explore what we believe and what it means to be part of our community.

Our Core Beliefs

We are committed to the faithful teaching of the Bible and hold to the historic creeds of the Christian faith. Our beliefs are foundational to everything we do, shaping our worship, our relationships, and our mission.

We are a member of the Evangelical Fellowship of Congregational Churches (EFCC) and our core doctrines align with theirs.
